Vocabulary Flashcards App

Click on the blurred content to reveal the answer. Click on the bolded head word to collapse a card. Flag a card as easy (green), medium (yellow), or hard (red) by clicking on the corresponding button. Card "collapsed" status and colours are saved in your browser (so you can close the tab and come back later, but if you clear your browser's cache, you'll lose your record, or if you use a different browser or device, your flags will not transfer over).
